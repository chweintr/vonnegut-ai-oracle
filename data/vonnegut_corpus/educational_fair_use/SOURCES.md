## Notes on Source Reliability

- **Project Gutenberg** and **Wikisource** are highly reliable for public domain texts
- **Educational institution websites** (Syracuse, HWS, etc.) provide authoritative commencement speech transcripts
- **Interview transcripts** from third-party sites should be cross-referenced when possible
- **The Progressive** and **PBS** are reputable original sources for interviews
- **Playboy interview** archived on multiple sites; appears to be accurate transcription

---

## Recommended Citation Format

When citing materials from this corpus, please cite the original source. Example:

> Vonnegut, Kurt. "Address to Graduating Class at Bennington College." 1970. Transcript available at http://jsomers.net/vonnegut-1970-commencement.html

For public domain works:

> Vonnegut, Kurt. "Harrison Bergeron." *The Magazine of Fantasy and Science Fiction*, October 1961. Public domain. Available at Project Gutenberg.
